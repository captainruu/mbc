About Us

More Than A Gym

Muscle Beach Club is Uluwatu's premier fitness destination, nestled in the heart of Bali's most iconic coastal region. We offer an elite combination of world-class facilities and a vibrant community atmosphere.

From an infrared sauna and ice bath recovery to pole dance studios and outdoor CrossFit, everything you need to reach your peak is here — surrounded by the beauty of Bali.
